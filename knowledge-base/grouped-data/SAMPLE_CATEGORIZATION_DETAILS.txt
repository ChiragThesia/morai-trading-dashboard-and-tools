## Methodology

1. Random stratified sample: 10 files from 2005, 15 from 2006, 15 from 2007
2. Manual read-through of first 300-500 characters of each file
3. Content analysis and categorization based on primary topic
4. Pattern identification from titles and content
5. Pattern validation against broader uncategorized set
6. Refinement of patterns through multiple passes

## Results

- 40 sample files categorized across 11 categories
- Patterns developed from sample applied to 5,217 uncategorized files
- ~70% categorization success rate (3,641 files)
- Identified key topic indicators in filename patterns
- Remaining 26% likely contain specialized or cross-disciplinary content
