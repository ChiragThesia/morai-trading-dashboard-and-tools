Wire the gate and brakes into the picker use-case and surface their state on the snapshot —
turning the pure functions from Plans 01/02 into the observable behavior PLAY-01 and PLAY-02
require: the picker computes nothing new to enter when the market says don't, and pauses on the
anti-criteria brakes, always naming the reason.

Following the existing eventsContextStatus → zeroEventAdjustment post-scoring-override
precedent, a new Step 3c reads the macro pair + open-calendar count + recent-closed rows, a new
Step 3d calls resolveEntryGate (seeded with the previous cycle's gate via the existing

score, and Step 6 overrides candidates to [] when blocked/blind/braked — while termStructure,
gex, and events stay populated so the board and Analyzer keep their context. The gate ships as
an additive PickerSnapshot.gate field, defaulted in the Zod schema so old stored rows parse.
