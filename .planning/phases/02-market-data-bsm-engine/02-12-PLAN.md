<objective>
Close UAT Gap C (truth: "Stored observation times reflect actual quote time (UTC) so
journal/DTE math is correct"). Production round-2 evidence on 2026-06-12 proved the CBOE
delayed-quotes `timestamp` field is already UTC: a payload stamped `2026-06-12 15:09:24`
arrived at 15:10:06 UTC wall clock (~60s delayed-quote skew). The Phase 2 RESEARCH
"Pitfall 1" premise — that the field is ET-local — was wrong. `etToUtc()` in cboe.ts
appends a -04:00/-05:00 offset on top of an already-UTC value, future-dating every stored
observation by exactly the EDT/EST offset (+4h observed live: max(time) 19:00:18Z while
wall clock was 15:08 UTC).

Fix the adapter to interpret the timestamp as UTC and delete the entire ET-conversion
machinery. Production evidence overrides the research pitfall; RESEARCH/PATTERNS docs
stay as historical record and are NOT edited.

Purpose: Correct journal/DTE math — every observation's time-to-expiry (T) feeds the BSM
greeks, so a +4h time error corrupts both the stored quote time AND the derived greeks.
Output: Corrected cboe.ts (UTC parse, no dead ET code) + flipped regression tests, plus a
documented post-deploy data-correction runbook for the orchestrator to execute against
production.
</objective>
