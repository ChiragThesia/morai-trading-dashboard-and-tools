| T-04-24 | Denial of Service | one expired app blocking the other | mitigate | Per-app state (D-09): market AUTH_EXPIRED → CBOE fallback; trader AUTH_EXPIRED → trader reads pause; the non-expired app keeps working; non-Schwab jobs untouched |
| T-04-25 | Tampering | invalid_grant misclassification | mitigate | toAppTokenStatus (04-01) + selectChainSource treat err/none-yet as non-fresh and fall back safely — never silently call an expired app |

</threat_model>
