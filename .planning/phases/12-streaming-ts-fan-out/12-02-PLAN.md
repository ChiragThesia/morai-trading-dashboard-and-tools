<objective>
Build the sidecar's live-stream engine: a background task that holds the GW-04 advisory lock,
opens a single Schwab StreamClient session from the trader client, subscribes to
LEVELONE_OPTIONS for open-position legs plus ad-hoc symbols, subscribes to ACCT_ACTIVITY for
fills, and forwards every event onto a module-level asyncio queue for the SSE endpoint (12-03)
to drain. Includes the SubscriptionManager that enforces the 490-symbol cap with LRU eviction
of ad-hoc symbols (D-05) and the position-reconcile diff that drives dynamic (un)subscription
(D-03). The stream is kept warm during RTH regardless of connected viewers (D-08).

Purpose: STRM-01 (LEVELONE) + STRM-02 (ACCT_ACTIVITY) live at the sole Schwab boundary, with
the undocumented ACCT_ACTIVITY types discovered empirically (logged, never filtered).
Output: apps/sidecar/streamer.py + pytest suite.
</objective>

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-12-02-01 | Information Disclosure | ACCT_ACTIVITY forwarding | mitigate | Strip the ACCOUNT field before pushing to event_queue (RESEARCH security row); account numbers never reach the browser |
| T-12-02-02 | Information Disclosure | error + activity logging | mitigate | Log type(exc).__name__ only; never log token values or raw account numbers (token_store V6 discipline) |
| T-12-02-03 | Elevation of Privilege | dual streamer session | mitigate | StreamClient.login() only inside the task that runs after app.state.has_lock (GW-04) — single-session guarantee (Pitfall 9) |
| T-12-02-04 | Denial of Service | event_queue growth | mitigate | asyncio.Queue(maxsize=500) bounds memory; 12-03 SSE drain + ping keepalive prevents unbounded backlog |
| T-12-SC | Tampering | pip installs | accept | Zero new PyPI packages (RESEARCH audit); sse-starlette explicitly rejected — StreamingResponse suffices |
</threat_model>
