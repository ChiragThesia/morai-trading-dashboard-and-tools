Fix the three cross-cutting mobile bugs that live outside the three screen bodies: (1) `MarketRail`'s
hardcoded `open` attribute (Pitfall 1) that makes the "collapsible" rail always-expanded and buries the
hero on phones (CONTEXT item 2); (2) `Shell`'s nav tabs whose 32px height is under the 44px touch

exact desktop behavior.

default closed and accept a `className` before Overview can reorder it, and the shell touch/safe-area/
`100dvh` corrections apply to all three screens at once (D-02, D-08, RESEARCH Pitfalls 1/3/5).

Output: MarketRail closed-by-default + `className`-driven + force-visible at `lg`; Shell nav ≥44px below
`lg` and `100dvh` main; AuthExpiredBanner safe-area-inset padding on both banner branches.
</objective>
